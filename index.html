About Me

My name is Nigel Joy. I use technologies and tools to solve problems.


My interests lean towards Web technologies. Apart from the usual necessary skills on HTML, CSS & Javascript, I am comfortable with Django & WordPress.


I prefer using Django to build custom Web applications. On the WordPress side of things, I provide technical support & guidance for WordPress users through my website ScratchTheWeb. I am also working on making some themes & plugins for WordPress.


Currently, I'm working as Technical Support Engineer at Inactiv.com Media Solutions Pvt Ltd where I help users of BlogVault, MalCare, WPRemote and Migrate Guru with the issues they face.


When I'm done with the work, I volunteer my time to help bloggers on the Official Blogger Forum where Google has recognized me as Product Expert and spend time on my side projects.
